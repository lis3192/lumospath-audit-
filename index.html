GLOSSARY

Plain English glossary

Bottleneck: The single step in a workflow where everything backs up. Fix this and the rest moves.
Owner load: How much of the work needs YOU specifically. High Owner load = hard to scale.
Flywheel: A system where each fix makes the next one cheaper. The opposite of a hamster wheel.
SOP: Standard Operating Procedure. A document or video that lets someone else do a task without you re-explaining.
VA: Virtual Assistant. A part-time contractor who handles tasks below your hourly rate.
AI assistant (e.g. ChatGPT, Claude): A writing tool that drafts in your voice once trained on examples. Like a junior copywriter who works in seconds.
Automation: A workflow that runs without you clicking buttons. Trigger fires, steps run, output appears.
Productisation: Turning custom 1:1 work into a repeatable package. Same core delivery, light tweaks per client.
